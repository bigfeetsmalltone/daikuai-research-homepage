Biography

I am come from Xingtai, Hebei. I am currently completing my Ph.D. in the Computer Science Dept. at Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) under the supervision of Prof. Yunming Ye . This is my final year of the research program. I will finish my Ph.D. defense and obtain my Ph.D. degree on around Dec. 2022.
I am looking for a suitable work or postdoctoral position, please feel free to contact me if you have a suitable position. My current research interests include a series of topics: few/zero-shot learning, meta learning, vision-language model, transfer learning with multiple models, cold-start recommendation, and data-limited drug discovery.
