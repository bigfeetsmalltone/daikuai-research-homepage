About Me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree in the Computer Science Dept. at Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) under the supervision of Prof. Xutao Li . This is my final year of the research program. I will finish my Ph.D. defense and obtain my Ph.D. degree on around Dec. 2024.
